Intro

My name is Gabriel Ferreira. I'm a resident of Rio De Janeiro, Brazil and live here with my aunt.

Currently, I work at Microlins as a Sales Manager, where I oversee sales strategies and team performance to achieve our business goals. Before this role, I was a professional basketball player, which allowed me to develop strong teamwork, leadership, and strategic thinking skills. I'm currently studying Systems Analysis and Development, which gives me a unique perspective on integrating technology and business operations.
